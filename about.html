About

Rajeev Polytechnic Hassan, a technical education institution in Karnataka, was established in 2009. The college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Karnataka. Rajeev Polytechnic Hassan offers diploma programmes in six branches of engineering: Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical and electronic Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, and Mechatronic Engineering. Each program has a duration of three years and follows the curriculum prescribed by the DTE, Karnataka.
